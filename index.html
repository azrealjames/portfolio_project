About me

First and foremost I would like to thank you for looking at my portfolio website.

My name is James Duran and I have been designing websites and learning more about the IT world for a couple of years now. I began studying in my own time and applied to a Udacity scholarship that I was awarded. The first part of the program was to learn about Frontend Web Development. During that time, I completed all of the tasks they had given me. I also helped with the yearbook project for our class. I am now currently doing the second part of the Udacity course which went from 10k plus students to around 2k or so to move on to the next round. I am very proud that I was accepted to the second part of the program.

With all of that being said, I really love the world of tech. Especially the designing part of it. I find it fascinating that you can take a html framework, use css to make it beautiful, and then use JavaScript to animate it.

The work is not all on me. I have a wife that pushes me to become more. She is the one that I can bounce ideas off of when I get stuck. She is also the one that helps me think outside the box when I need help and I owe her a great deal of thanks for being my wife but for helping me see what I sometimes can't.
